The Science Behind Animal Face Analysis

Our Animal Face Test isn't just a random guesser. It uses advanced computer vision and machine learning to analyze the geometric features of your face.

1. Facial Landmark Detection

We use a pre-trained model to identify 68 specific points on the face, including the eyes, nose, mouth, and jawline. This mapping is done entirely in your browser using TensorFlow.js.

2. Feature Extraction

The model calculates ratios, such as the distance between the eyes relative to the width of the face, the sharpness of the jawline, and the height of the forehead.

3. Neural Network Classification

These features are compared against thousands of "archetypal" images for various animal types (Dog, Cat, Rabbit, etc.). The final percentage result is the probability that your face matches one of these archetypes.

Why Privacy Matters

Because this happens in the browser, your image never leaves your computer. This is the gold standard for privacy in AI applications.
