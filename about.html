About AnimalFaceTest

AnimalFaceTest is a free, privacy-first AI entertainment platform that helps people discover their animal face type. Using advanced machine learning technology, we analyze facial features to determine which animal archetype — Dog, Cat, Horse, Raccoon Dog, Koala, or Panda — best matches a person's facial structure.

Our Technology

We utilize TensorFlow.js and Google Teachable Machine to run neural network inference directly in the user's browser. Our classification model is trained on a diverse dataset of facial archetypes, examining factors such as:

  • Eye shape and angle (rounded vs. sharp/upward-slanting)
  • Jawline contour (soft and rounded vs. angular and defined)
  • Facial proportions and aspect ratio
  • Nose bridge width and profile
  • Overall facial silhouette and balance

Because the model runs locally, there is zero latency from server communication and zero risk of your biometric data being exposed.

The Cultural Tradition Behind Animal Face Types

The concept of animal face archetypes has deep roots in East Asian culture, particularly in Korean and Japanese beauty and entertainment communities. Historically derived from the ancient practice of physiognomy — reading personality through facial features — it has been modernized and popularized through K-beauty trends, social media, and entertainment.

Today, "강아지상 (dog face)" and "고양이상 (cat face)" are common compliments in Korean culture, each carrying rich personality associations. Our platform brings this cultural concept to a global audience through the lens of modern AI.
