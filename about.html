About AnimalFaceTest

AnimalFaceTest is a cutting-edge platform that combines Artificial Intelligence with fun personality trends. Founded in 2026, our mission is to provide engaging AI experiences while prioritizing user privacy above all else.

Our Technology

We utilize advanced neural networks and computer vision models (TensorFlow.js) to analyze facial archetypes. Our models are trained on diverse datasets to recognize the subtle nuances that define "Dog," "Cat," and other animal face types.

Privacy First

We believe the future of the web is decentralized and private. That's why our analysis happens on YOUR device, not our servers.
