Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 15, 2026

AnimalFaceTest ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website animalfacetest.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it. Please read this policy carefully.

1. Who We Are

We are the operators of AnimalFaceTest, an AI-powered entertainment website that helps users identify their animal face type.

  • Website: https://animalfacetest.com
  • Contact Email: admin@animalfacetest.com

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Photo / Image Data

We do not collect, upload, or store your photos. When you use the Animal Face Test, your photo is analyzed entirely within your own browser using TensorFlow.js. The image data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect the following information through our analytics and advertising services:

  • IP address (anonymized where required by law)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or source
  • Device type and screen resolution
  • Interactions with the website (clicks, scrolls, mouse movements)

2.3 Contact Form Data

If you submit our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message content. This data is processed by Formspree (formspree.io) and is subject to their privacy policy.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Site Analytics: To understand how users interact with our website and improve our content and user experience (via Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity).
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements on our website through Google AdSense. These ads may be personalized based on your browsing history and interests if you have consented to advertising cookies.
  • Security: To monitor for fraudulent activity and protect the integrity of our website.
  • Communication: To respond to inquiries submitted through our contact form.

5. Third-Party Services and Advertising

5.1 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting aboutads.info. Google's use of advertising cookies is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5.2 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymized usage data. Google Analytics uses cookies to track user interactions. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. Learn more at Google's Privacy Policy.

5.3 Microsoft Clarity

We use Microsoft Clarity, a behavioral analytics tool that records session replays and generates heatmaps. Microsoft Clarity uses cookies and may collect data about your interactions with our site. For more information, see Microsoft's Privacy Statement.

5.4 Userback

We use Userback to collect user feedback. If you submit feedback, your message and associated data will be processed by Userback in accordance with their privacy policy at userback.io/privacy-policy.

5.5 Formspree

Our contact form is processed by Formspree. Submissions are subject to Formspree's Privacy Policy.

6. Personalized Advertising and AdChoices

We participate in interest-based advertising. Our advertising partners, including Google, may use cookies and similar technologies to show you ads based on your interests across websites. You have the following options to control personalized advertising:

  • Google Ads Settings – Manage your Google advertising preferences
  • DAA AdChoices – Opt out of interest-based advertising (US)
  • Your Online Choices – Opt out of interest-based advertising (EU)
  • The cookie consent banner on our website – Decline advertising cookies

7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data:

7.1 GDPR Rights (EEA, UK residents)

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time by adjusting your cookie preferences.

7.2 CCPA Rights (California residents)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected and how it is used.
  • Right to delete personal information we have collected.
  • Right to opt out of the sale of personal information. (We do not sell personal information.)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at admin@animalfacetest.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Our content is family-friendly and does not contain adult material.
