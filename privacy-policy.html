Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to AnimalFaceTest. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data, specifically emphasizing our local-first AI processing.

2. Photo Privacy (Critical)

We do not upload your photos. Unlike other services, our Animal Face Test uses TensorFlow.js to process images directly in your browser. Your facial data never leaves your device and is never stored on our servers.

3. Information We Collect

We collect standard log files (IP addresses, browser type) for security and analytical purposes. We also use Google Analytics to understand site traffic and Google AdSense to serve advertisements.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience and to serve personalized ads through Google AdSense. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at admin@animalfacetest.com.
