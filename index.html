Comprehensive Guide to the Animal Face Test

The Animal Face Test is a popular personality and facial analysis trend that categorizes human facial features into different animal types. Originating in East Asia, this trend has evolved with AI technology to provide deeper insights into how facial proportions influence perceived personality traits.

🐶 The Dog Face Type (Friendly & Loyal)

People with a dog face type typically have rounder facial features, big eyes, and a soft, friendly expression. In terms of facial geometry, they often have a more rounded chin and a shorter mid-face. They are often perceived as loyal, energetic, and approachable. This face type is associated with a warm and trustworthy personality that makes others feel comfortable instantly.

Common traits: Warm gaze, expressive eyes, soft jawline.

🐱 The Cat Face Type (Chic & Mysterious)

The cat face type is characterized by sharp, upward-slanting eyes, a prominent jawline, and a more chic or mysterious aura. Geometrically, this type often features a V-line chin and a defined nose. People with this type are often seen as independent, intelligent, and charismatic. They may seem "cold" at first but possess an irresistible charm.

Common traits: Sharp eyes, high cheekbones, charismatic presence.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What is the difference between a Dog Face and a Cat Face?

A: A Dog Face is generally characterized by rounder features and a friendly, approachable look. A Cat Face has sharper, more defined lines and a sophisticated, chic aura. Our AI model analyzes these visual archetypes.
