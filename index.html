What is the Animal Face Test?

The Animal Face Test is a popular personality and facial analysis trend that categorizes human facial features into different animal types. The most common types are Dog Face and Cat Face.

🐶 The Dog Face Type

People with a dog face type typically have rounder facial features, big eyes, and a soft, friendly expression. They are often perceived as loyal, energetic, and approachable. This face type is associated with a warm and trustworthy personality.

🐱 The Cat Face Type

The cat face type is characterized by sharp, upward-slanting eyes, a prominent jawline, and a more chic or mysterious aura. People with this type are often seen as independent, intelligent, and charismatic.

How It Works

Our tool uses TensorFlow.js and Teachable Machine AI models trained on thousands of images to identify patterns and features associated with these animal types. All analysis happens right in your browser, ensuring your data remains private.

About This Project

AnimalFaceTest was created to provide a fun and interactive way to explore AI technology. We believe in transparency and user privacy. No photos are uploaded to any server; everything is processed locally on your device.
